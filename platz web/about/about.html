ABOUT

Platz Stencil was designed through the use of Futura medium. The grid for the cuts depends where the strokes would meet.
I was inspired by the typefaces of Bifur and _, which gave me the idea of adding a few lines to play with nagative space.
Like bifur I wanted to have at least one line that stayed attached to the typeface. This is the case with the top outer line.
The middle line is to show the eveness of the typrface itself. Just as Futura whose structure is measured evenly into "perfection".
